## Prompt 05 — Build the Primary Demonstration

Create a staged but genuinely interactive response sequence.

Onyx should produce a structured answer containing:

1. Begin with the person
   - understand why they came in
   - listen before recommending anything

2. Ask approved discovery questions
   - prior experience
   - preferred format
   - desired experience
   - comfort level
   - relevant concerns within company policy

3. Explain clearly
   - avoid overwhelming language
   - distinguish general education from medical advice
   - confirm understanding

4. Maintain boundaries
   - do not diagnose
   - do not promise outcomes
   - involve the appropriate licensed professional when required
   - follow TAS policy and applicable regulations

5. Suggested knowledge to review
   - First-Time Patient Service Standard
   - Product Formats Overview
   - Cannabinoid Basics
   - Escalation and Pharmacist Support Guidance

The answer should include:

- approved-source indicators
- knowledge links
- confidence or verification treatment
- next recommended action
- a concise disclaimer
- feedback controls such as “Helpful” or “Open source”

Do not fabricate detailed regulations or medical instructions.

Validate the response flow, source interactions, mobile reading experience, and keyboard behavior.
